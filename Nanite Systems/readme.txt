scripts to work with Nanite System units in Second Life

http://develop.nanite-systems.com


charger.lsl is a wireless charger that works on the owner only

sniff.lsl is a light bus listener

scanner.lsl is a unit scaner, holdntext.lsl goes along with it
scanner goes into the root prim, where the cable comes from, holdntext goes into a second prim to handle the hold animation and the hovertext
